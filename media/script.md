Janulus method - named after powell 48 languages using this method. 
Focusses on pronunciation, grammar , and building mind-muscle connections to get you start speaking asap.

This process is surprisingly simple, You take a verb and noun clause and iterate through different tenses.
Under standing language strucutre of tenses, plurality.

Our app takes this further, and allows the user to generate their won janulus matrix based on their own vocabulary.
They can choose the tenses, and other grammatical options they want to practice.

Currently we support over 80 languages using google gemini as a grammar engine, and google-translate/eleven labs to generate audio for your cards so you can practice pronunciation.
Happy language learning.
